A free companion for PowerScribe®

If you use PowerScribe, you should be using this.

Dictum runs beside PowerScribe and upgrades it in three ways. It installs in five minutes with no IT ticket, and nothing you dictate ever leaves your workstation.

  • Dictation that never drifts. Press F9 (or the PowerMic button already in your hand) and a modern offline speech engine puts the words straight into the open report. No voice profile to train, corrupt, or retrain: the same sentence produces the same text, every time.

  • Impressions draft themselves. Press F10 and an on-device medical AI drafts the IMPRESSION from the findings you just dictated. Your own words, placed in the report as a draft for your review.

  • Autocomplete that knows your reports. Dictum learns your phrasing and finishes your sentences right in PowerScribe. It imports your own AutoText in one click.

Sound familiar?

Built against PowerScribe's oldest complaints

“I dictated the exact same sentence I say twenty times a day. Different text. Again.”

Say it the same, get the same

Press F9 (or the PowerMic button already in your hand) and the words land straight in the open report. The engine is a modern transformer (Parakeet-TDT) with no voice profile to train, drift, or corrupt: the same utterance produces the same text. It also decodes about 10× faster than you speak on an ordinary CPU, so you'll never watch the recognizer catch up.

“It still doesn't know the words I use every single day.”

Autocomplete, right in the report

Start a word in PowerScribe and a suggestion popup follows your caret: Tab accepts, Esc dismisses. Dictum quietly learns your vocabulary and phrasing from the reports you write, locally, on disk you control, so after a few days your own sentences finish themselves. Import your own PowerScribe AutoText in one click; a curated radiology corpus covers the rest.

“I just spent five minutes describing it. Now I get to summarize it all over again.”

Impressions draft themselves

Press F10 and an on-device medical language model drafts the IMPRESSION from the findings you just dictated: a diagnostic synthesis of your own words, never a finding you didn't make and never an unsolicited recommendation. It lands in the report as a draft for your review; you edit and sign exactly as you do today.

Drop-in, not rip-out

PowerScribe and Dragon stay exactly as they are. Every Dictum feature is a checkbox: untick them all and you're back to stock in one second.

PowerMic ready

The record button on your existing PowerMic drives Dictum, press-and-hold or toggle, your choice. No new hardware on the desk.

Made for reading rooms

A compact, always-on-top panel in a warm, low-glare theme that sits politely next to your PACS monitors and stays out of the way.

For you, and for your IT team

Every claim on this page is checkable

Signed binaries

The installer and app are Authenticode-signed (Microsoft Azure Trusted Signing), and every release publishes its SHA-256 in latest.json. Updates verify both before installing.

Per-user install, nothing system-wide

Installs to %LOCALAPPDATA%\Dictum like Chrome or Zoom user installs: no admin rights, no services, no drivers, no system changes. Uninstalls cleanly from Windows Settings → Apps.

What touches the network

Two things: downloading the app and its AI models (from dictum.report, GitHub, Hugging Face, nuget.org), and a once-daily version check. Dictation audio and report text are processed on the workstation and are never transmitted. There is no account, no telemetry, no analytics.

No BAA required

A business-associate agreement covers vendors that receive PHI. We receive none, by architecture: there is no server that could see your data. The privacy statement is the reference document for your compliance office.
